About Me

All About David

Hello! My name is King David G. Aguilon, born on June 19, 2003. I graduated from ACLC College Batangas and am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Batangas State University.

I live in Sambat, San Pascual, Batangas, with my parents, elder siblings, younger brother, and younger sister. My father is a company driver, working hard to support our family, while my mother takes care of our home.

My hobbies include watching anime, K-dramas, and Thai dramas. Anime, in particular, has taught me valuable lessons about perseverance, relationships, and human nature. I also enjoy drawing, a passion I developed in elementary school thanks to my sister, who introduced me to art and anime.
